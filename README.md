## 🚀 What I'm Building

### 🛡️ Email Validation & Disposable Email Detector
Protect your SaaS signup flow from fake accounts and disposable emails.
Detects 500+ temporary email providers, validates MX records, and scores risk levels.

[![Try on Apify](https://img.shields.io/badge/Try%20on-Apify-1ABC9C?style=for-the-badge)](https://apify.com/mayno/email-risk-scorer)

**What it does:**
- 🚫 Detects 500+ disposable providers (Mailinator, Guerrilla Mail, etc.)
- ✅ Validates MX records
- 📊 Risk scoring (low / medium / high)
- 📧 Free vs business email classification
- 💰 ~$1 per 1,000 checks
